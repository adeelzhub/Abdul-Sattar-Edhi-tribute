Abdul Sattar Edhi

"The Angel Of Mercy"

  • Abdul Sattar Edhi (28 February 1928 – 8 July 2016) was a Pakistani philanthropist, ascetic, and humanitarian who founded the Edhi Foundation.
  • He setup world’s largest volunteer ambulance network.
  • Edhi was registered as a parent or guardian of nearly 20,000 adopted children of whom he was an active caretaker.
  • His organisation runs various homeless shelters, animal shelters, rehabilitation centres, and orphanages across Pakistan.
